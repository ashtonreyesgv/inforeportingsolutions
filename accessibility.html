Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making this website usable by the widest possible audience.

At Tax Information Reporting Initiative, LLC doing business as InfoReporting Solutions, we believe everyone should be able to access information with ease and confidence. We are committed to making inforeportingsolutions.com accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability.

Our website has been developed with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) in mind. This means we aim to provide features such as:

  • Compatibility with common browsers and devices at different screen sizes
  • Consistent navigation
  • Descriptive alternative text for images
  • Proper use of color and contrast for readability
  • Support for keyboard navigation and screen readers

We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing process. While many of our pages meet WCAG standards, there may be areas that can be improved. We welcome your feedback to help us continue enhancing the experience for all visitors.

Need help or want to report an issue?

If you have trouble accessing any part of our website, or if you need accommodation due to a disability, please let us know. We will work with you to ensure you have full access to the information, resources, and services you need.
